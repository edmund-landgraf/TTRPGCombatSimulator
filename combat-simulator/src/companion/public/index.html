PCs (JSON)

Array of combatants, or one object. Side is forced to party. Assign a primary tactics group and optional secondary (e.g. frontliner + battlefield control).

    Enemies (JSON)

    Array of combatants, or one object. Side is forced to enemy. Primary + optional secondary tactics, same catalog as PCs.

      Map

      Square grid (Size × Size). Auto-place puts enemies on the north edge and party on the south edge. Drag tokens to adjust before you run.

      Deployment

      Load sample or import combatants — then drag tokens on the grid.
